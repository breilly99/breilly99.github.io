Welcome!

Hello and welcome to my website!

My name is Brendan Reilly and I am a graduating senior at the University of Miami.

I am a broadcast journalism major and the Sports Director for WVUM 90.5FM, the University of Miami's student radio station.

Over my four years at Miami, I have broadcasted over 150 Miami Hurricanes sporting events, ranging from baseball, to men's and women's basketball and football.

Upon my graduation from the University of Miami, I will be moving to Longview, Washington for the summer, calling baseball for the Cowlitz Black Bears of the West Coast League.

I got my start in broadcasting when I was young when I would mute the television and broadcast over the video games I was playing such as Madden and MLB The Show.

Little did I know that I would be pursuing it as a career in my future.
